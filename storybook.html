The crew

Storybook

Every story needs its heroes. Tap a kitty card to read a little more about them.

⚖️

Baker

The Judge. Sassy matriarch, permanently unimpressed.

Heart-shaped nose, two different green eyes, and a stare that has never once been impressed. Baker raised a whole litter of kittens on her own before it was finally her turn to be the baby. She greets the world like she owns it — because she does. Look for her gavel.

⭐

Doc

The Bailiff. Gentle giant, certified love bug.

The rounder, the better. Doc comes when his name is called and has a different meow for food, kibble, and water — a cat with his own sign language. A mighty little voice that refused to be unchosen, now the big brother of the whole house. Never far from his sparkly pom pom.

🌿

Lulu

The Dramatic One. Garden princess, ultimate sweetheart.

She appeared out of the garden like a mystery — the family blamed a raccoon for weeks. Won over instantly by a pom pom, home before anyone even knew it. Presses a paw to her chest when emoting, eyes theatrically closed. Has learned to operate the air conditioner. Really.

🔔

Ilona

The Chaos Agent. Never once worried.

Perfect circle head, bell collar, a floating question mark above her at all times. Ilona was staring at a wall before her court date and will return to it afterward. Demands to be paid in respirations. Volunteers for overtime snuggles. Doc’s little sister and proud of it.
